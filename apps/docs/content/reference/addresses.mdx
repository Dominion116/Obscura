The mock underlying tokens are freely mintable on Sepolia. The Obscura app's
**Faucet** page mints them to any connected wallet so you always have
something to wrap.

## Verifying for yourself

Both the registry and the wrappers are UUPS proxies whose implementations are
verified on Sourcify (exact match). The ABIs in
[`packages/shared/src/abis`](https://github.com/Dominion116/Obscura/tree/main/packages/shared/src/abis)
were extracted from those verified implementations, and the official list is
maintained in the
[Zama docs](https://docs.zama.org/protocol/protocol-apps/addresses/testnet/sepolia).
