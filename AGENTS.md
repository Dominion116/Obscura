This project (Obscura) is being built for the Zama Developer Program, Mainnet Season 3, Bounty Track. The official bounty listing is the source of truth for scope, rules, and judging criteria:

https://forms.zama.org/developer-program-mainnet-season3-bounty-track

Check this link whenever uncertain whether a proposed change is in scope, out of scope, or otherwise conflicts with the bounty requirements. If [implementation.md](implementation.md) and the bounty page appear to disagree, the bounty page wins since it is the original source the PRD was derived from.
